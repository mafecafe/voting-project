Democracy in the age
of artificial intelligence

In 2024, over 70 countries held national elections while AI-generated deepfakes, synthetic audio, and fabricated imagery became widely accessible. This chart maps internet penetration across 200 countries—the higher the bar, the greater the share of the population online. Countries holding elections are highlighted in color by region. Where connectivity is highest, the surface for AI-driven disinformation is largest, making these democracies especially vulnerable.
